Our story

Owl was founded by Alexandros Prassas and Ronaldo Dervishaj (graduates of the "Athens University of Economics and Business") in 2015 with a vision of providing life-transforming learning experiences to learners around the world. Today, Owl is a global online learning platform that offers anyone,anywhere,access to online courses and degrees from leading universities and companies. Owl received B Corp certification in February 2021, which means that we have a legal duty not only to our shareholders, but to also make a positive impact on society more broadly, as we continue our efforts to reduce barriers to world-class education for all.
